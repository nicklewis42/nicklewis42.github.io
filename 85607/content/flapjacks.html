Flapjacks

DescriptionQuantityUOMComment
Light brown sugar125g
butter125g
Porridge oats250g
Golden syrup95g

Method

Process oats to break them down a little, don't try to turn them into a flour you want to retain some oaty texture.

Thoroughly mix the oats and sugar in a bowl.

Melt the butter and syrup together in a pan, not too hot, just enough to melt the butter.

Add the melted butter and syrup to the bowl and mix well.

Press into a baking tray and score into squares.

Bake for about 15 minutes at gas 6.
