Eating Apples 

Method

Roll out the puff pastry about 3-4 mm thick

Cut to size, either for individual or a large one. Rectangles are fine and simple to do.

Brush the pastry with egg, then put a layer of apple puree or very finely chopped apple on the pastry, leave about 15-20mm border.

Now layer thin slices of apple across the are that has the puree bed.

Sprinkle with caster sugar and pour over melted butter. Now brush the edges with egg.

Bake for about 20-25 minutes at gas 7.
