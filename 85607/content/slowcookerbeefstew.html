Slow cooker beef stew

Introduction

This recipe does not need to be precise you can adjust the quantities if you like, a lot of it is about what you feel like and what you have/can get. Add any other veg you fancy.

DescriptionQuantityUOMComment
diced beef400gEither buy it diced or dice it yourself, cheaper cuts are fine
Dried Onion1Small handful
Rich Beef stock500ml
Red wine1miniature bottle
Corn flour2rounded desert spoon
Potatoes600g
Carrots250g

Method

Peel and chop the root veg into the sizes you like, carrots smaller potatoes bigger.

Put the stock, root veg and onions into the slow cooker.

Thoroughly sear the beef all over in a very hot pan to develop the flavour.

Add the beef to the slow cooker.

Deglaze the pan with the red wine and add this to the slow cooker.

Cook on low for ten hours.

45 minutes before serving slake the cornflour with water then mix with a little of the liquid in the cooker.

Add the cornflour mix to the cooker and give the stew a good stir.

If you have any fragile veg to add such as peas or butter beans add them at the same time as the cornflour.

Final Comments

Classic beef stew, it only gets better the longer you cook it!
