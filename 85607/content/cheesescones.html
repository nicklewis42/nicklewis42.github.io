Cheese Scones

DescriptionQuantityUOMComment
SR flour340g
butter83g
grated cheese250g
salt1pinch
mustard powder level tsp

Method

Add salt and mustard to flour

Rub in butter until fine and crumbly

Mix in cheese, best by hand

Add a little milk until it bind into a dough

Roll out and cut

Glaze with milk or beaten egg

Bake for 15 minutes at gas 7
