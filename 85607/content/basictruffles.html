Basic Truffles

Introduction

A basic truffle mix, add alost anything to flavour it.

DescriptionQuantityUOMComment
Dark Chocolate100g
Double Cream36g
Unsalted butter50g

Method

Break chocolate into small pieces in a glass bowl and add the cream

Melt over simmering water

Chop the butter into small pieces and add it to the mix along with any flavouring

Stir until silky and smooth

Chill for at least two hours

Divide into individual truffles, toss them in vermicelli, cocoa or icing sugar

Chill, again preferably overnight
