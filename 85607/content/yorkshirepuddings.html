Yorkshire Puddings

Introduction

Makes big puffy yorkshires, scale as required.

A couple of things are vital for success, make sure the oven, tray and oil are really hot and don't open the oven door during cooking.

DescriptionQuantityUOMComment
Plain Flour66g
eggs1ea
Milk100ml

Method

Stir the egg into the flour.

Gently whisk in the milk a little at a time and whisk to a smooth batter.

Leave the batter in the fridge for at least 30 minutes, ideally overnight.

Preheat the oven to Gas 7 then put a good pool of oil into the bottom of four cups in a yorkshire tray.

Take the hot tray and put 1/4 of the mix in each cup, put it straight back in the oven.

Cook for twenty five minutes, then check through the oven door, they might need a little longer.
