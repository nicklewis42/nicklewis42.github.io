Roast Chicken Breast

DescriptionQuantityUOMComment
Chicken Breast2EachMed size breasts
Smoked paprika TeaspoonUnsmoked is fine if you prefer
Soft brown sugar2teaspoon
Salt and pepper 

Method

Preheat the oven to gas 7.

Mix the sugar and paprika and season with salt and pepper to taste.

Rub the chicken breasts all over with the seasoning.

Roast for 20 minutes, tweak the time a bit depending on thickness of breasts.

Finish the breasts under a hot grill for a minute or two a side.

Slice the breasts at an angle so they present like a traditional roast dinner.
