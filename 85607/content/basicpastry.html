Basic Pastry

Introduction

A basic savoury pastry for quiche, pies etc.

DescriptionQuantityUOMComment
egg yolk1ea
plain flour190g
Butter110gThis should be cold and chopped into chunks
Water4tspCold

Method

Rub the butter into the flour to get a breadcrumbs texture.

Add the egg yolk and water and bring together, first with a knife and then your hands

Chill for a bit before rolling out, then chill again before blind baking.
