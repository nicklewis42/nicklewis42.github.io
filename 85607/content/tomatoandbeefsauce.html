Tomato and beef Sauce

Introduction

Call it Bolognaise if you like!!

Serve it in lasagne or with spaghetti.

DescriptionQuantityUOMComment
Minced beef400gThe leaner the better
Red Wine200ml
Chopped tomato1pack
Plain flour2dsp

Method

Cook the mince in the saucepan on a fairly high heat to brown it uniformly. Make sure the mince is broken up into fine pieces. Remove most, not all, of the fat and discard it.

Scatter in the flour to thicken and stir in.

Add the tomatoes and wine.

Simmer for 2 hours to develop the flavour add a little boiling water if required to keep the correct consistency.
