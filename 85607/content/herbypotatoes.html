Method

Peel and cube the potatoes, we are looking for cubes of about 1" or maybe a little smaller.

Boil the potatoes for four minutes and then drain and allow to dry.

Air fry the potatoes at 200 degrees, check and shake them every five minutes or so.

When the potatoes are nicely browened and crips they are ready to serve.

Final Comments

Final cooking time seems to vary quite a bit, plan for fifteen to twenty minutes and see how it goes.
