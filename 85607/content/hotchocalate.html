Hot chocalate

Introduction

Hot chocalate made from a bar, make it in a jug, if you try to do it in a mug you'll spill it.

DescriptionQuantityUOMComment
Cadburys Dairy milk25g
Semi Skimmed Milk240g

Method

Weigh the stuff into a jug, one that will only be half full.

Microwave on full for 2 min 40 sec

Blend it and then froth it with a milk frother.

Final Comments

For two mugs double the weights and microwave for five minutes.
