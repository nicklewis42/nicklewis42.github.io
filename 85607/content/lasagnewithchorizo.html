Lasagne with Chorizo

Introduction

Lightly spiced lasagne bolognaise with home made pasta.

DescriptionQuantityUOMComment
eggs5ea
00 flour As much as needed for the eggs
finely minced beef500g
Passata500g
Chorizo sausage22 Med size sausages but to taste
Grated cheese For topping

Method

Mix flour into the eggs until a soft dough is formed. This needs to be refrigerated for at least an hour.

Brown the mince.

Chop the sausages into small pieces, similar to the minced beef pieces.

Fry the chorizo until well browned.

Add a little water to the pan and boil to incorporate all the chorizo residue.

Mix water, chorizo, beef and passata but keep a little passata back.

Assemble the lasagne with a thin smear of neat passata then pasta rolled out really thin, meat mixture, pasta, meat mixture, pasta and top with the last of the passata followed by a gernerous covering of cheese.

Bake in the oven gas 4 for forty minutes or so until well browned.

Final Comments

Should provide three generous portions.

Shop bought pasta is fine if you prefer.
