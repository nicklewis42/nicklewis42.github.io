White sauce corn flour thckened

Introduction

This is a basic white sauce base thickened with corn flour, add parsley, cheese or whatever to customise it.

DescriptionQuantityUOMComment
Corn Flour2dstsprounded
Milk350ml

Method

Slake the cornflour with enough liquid to make it pour easily.

Bring the milk to boiling keep it on a very low heat.

Carefully whisk in the corn flour mix, keep whisking to prevent lumps.

Gently heat without boiling until it thickens.

Final Comments

This is a basic fairly thick white sauce, tweak the proportions for the kind of result you are after.

If you are making a cheese sauce a little mustard and salt brings out the flavour.
