Soda Bread

DescriptionQuantityUOMComment
SR flour350g
Bicarbonate of Soda1teaspoon
Salt1tsp
Butter Milk285gAlernatively use whole milk and replace 20g with lemon juice

Method

Preheat the oven and a baking sheet to gas 4.

Mix flour, bicarb and salt in a large bowl.

Add the liquid and blend with a knife until it comes together as a very sticky dough.

Lightly flour the baking sheet and drop the ball of dough in the middle.

Roughly shape the dough and cut a large cross almost right through the full depth.

Bake it for 40 minutes.
