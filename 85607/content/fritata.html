Fritata

Introduction

A kind of caseless quiche cooked in a frying pan. Like a quiche the fillings can be pretty much anything although the peas and potatoes are traditional ingedients. Fillings should all be cooked before the eggs are added. Quantites should work in a medium sized pan and serve three or four. Note you need an oven proof frying pan for this.

Method

Peel the potatoes and cut into half inch cubes.

Other than the eggs cream and cheese everything needs to be cooked before the eggs are added so boil/steam the potatoes and peas, bacon veg etc. can be precooked or sauted in the pan before adding the eggs.

At about this point you should be putting the oven on at Gas 4, you don't wnat to put the pan into a cold oven.

Add everything except the cheese, eggs and cream to the hot pan, anything that needs cooking should go in first, fragile things (like peas) last.

While everything is still hot distribute the cooked meats and some chopped or grated cheese in the pan.

Beat the cream and eggs together and pour into the hot pan.

Heat on the hob until starting to cook round the edges then the pan goes into the preheated (Gas 4) oven.

Cook for about thirty minutes then turn over onto a plate slice and serve. DO NOT FORGET THE PAN HANDLE IS HOT!!!
