Pan Fried Mackeral

Introduction

Delightful light moist fish.

DescriptionQuantityUOMComment
Mackeral Fillets, skin on. One per person as a starter, maybe two for a generous main.

Method

Get your frying pan really hot.

Add a little oil to the pan.

Place the mackeral in the pan skin side down and immediately drop to a gentle heat.

Fry for 10 minutes - don't touch!

Turn carefully and fry for a further five minutes.

Plate up and rest for a minute or two.
