Fruit Cake

DescriptionQuantityUOMComment
sr flour225g
butter110g
Caster sugar110g
eggs1
Milk 
dried fruit225g
Mixed spice1tsp

Method

Blend flour and butter to breadcrumbs.

Add sugar and spice.

Add the egg and enough milk for a firm dropping consistency.

Fold the fruit into mixture.

Put in a lined tin and Bake at gas 4 for 45mins to an hour.
