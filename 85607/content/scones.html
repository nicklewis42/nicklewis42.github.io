Scones

DescriptionQuantityUOMComment
SR flour350g
Baking powder1teaspoon
Butter85g
Caster sugar42g
Salt teaspoon
Milk175ml
Vanilla extract1teaspoon
Lemon juice1teaspoon

Method

Preheat the oven to gas 7.

Mix flour and baking powder in a large bowl.

Rub in butter until fine and crumbly.

Stir in the sugar.

Heat the milk in the microwave for 30 seconds to make it slightly warm.

Add the lemon juice and vanilla to the milk.

Put your baking sheet in the oven.

Make a well in the dry mixture and pour in all the liquid.

Combine the mix quickly with a table knife.

Scatter some flour onto the work surface and turn out the dough, fold the dough over two or three times, do not knead it.

Pat the dough into a round (don't roll it) and then cut out your scones say 5cm across and 2 or 3 deep as you wish.

You can gently recombine the offcuts and cut a few more.

Glaze with milk or beaten egg.

Bake for 10 minutes at gas 7.
