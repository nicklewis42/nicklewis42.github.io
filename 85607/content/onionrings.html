Onion rings

Introduction

The batter can be used to fritter anything really but works great with onion rings.

DescriptionQuantityUOMComment
Onion1eaIt
SR flour150g
Sparkling water180g

Method

whisk the water and flour together, this will make quite a thick batter.

Slice the middle part of the onion into 10mm deep discs and separate into rings.

Dip the rings in the batter and allow excess to drain for a few seconds.

Drop into oil at 170 C for 2.5 minutes.

Floating rings (they should float) can be flipped over with a minute to go.
