Risotto base

Introduction

This recipe is for the base add peas, chicken, mushrooms etc. etc... About enough for 2 or 3.

If you want to do this in a slow cooker use 150g rice to 600g stock and cook on high, it should take about an hour and a half.

Method

Prepare the stock and bring it to the boil then leave the pan on a very low heat.

Finely chop the onions.

Melt the first knob of butter in a saucepan with a splash of oil and add the onions.

Add the rice and continue to fry, the rice should "crack" but do not allow it to burn.

Now add the wine, it should boil but will cool the pan.

Now patience is required, keep gently on the simmer, watch it like a hawk and stir very often. Keep adding the stock a ladel at a time each time it is absorbed.

Add the second knob of butter and stir, as soon as this has been incorporated it

Serve and scatter the cheese on top, for the purist this will be parmesan.

Final Comments

Along the way you will also have to add any other ingeredients, unless they require very little cooking it is best to cook them first.
