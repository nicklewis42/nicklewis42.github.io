Cherry Cake

Introduction

Scale to the number of eggs you want. For small individual cakes you should get 6 per egg, small cakes will need 20 to 25 minutes at gas 4.

DescriptionQuantityUOMComment
Glace cherries300g
SR four200g
Salt1pinch
butter175gAt room temperature
Caster sugar175g
Eggs3ea
Ground Almonds75gReplace with more flour if you don't have them
Milk75ml
Vanilla extract10ml

Method

Preheat the oven to gas mark 4

Line or grease your loaf tin

Halve or quarter the cherries wash and dry, smaller pieces are less prone to sinking

Mix everything except the cherries to make your batter

Fold in the cherries

Bake for 60 to 75 minutes until a skewer comes out clean

Leave in the tin for 15 minutes before turning out to cool
