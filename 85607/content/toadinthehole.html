Toad in the Hole

DescriptionQuantityUOMComment
Water50ml
Milk75ml
Plain flour75g
Egg1
Sausages4(or maybe six?)

Method

Beat flour, egg, milk and water to make batter.

Bake sausages for 10 minutes at Gas 7 in middle of oven.

Add a little oil if not much fat from sausages and return to top shelf for 5 mins.

Add batter and bake at the top of the oven for 30 mins turning down to five after ten minutes.
