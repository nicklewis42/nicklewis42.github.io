Cherry Cake (for two eggs)

Introduction

Scale to the number of eggs you want. For small individual cakes you should get 6 per egg, small cakes will need 20 to 25 minutes at gas 4 or 15 minutes in the air fryer at 150C.

DescriptionQuantityUOMComment
Glace cherries200g
SR four135g
Salt1small pinch
butter115gAt room temperature
Caster sugar115g
Eggs2ea
Ground Almonds50gReplace with more flour if you don't have them
Milk50ml
Vanilla extract5ml

Method

Preheat the oven to gas mark 4

Line or grease your loaf tin

Halve or quarter the cherries wash and dry, smaller pieces are less prone to sinking

Mix everything except the cherries to make your batter

Fold in the cherries

Bake for 50 to 65 minutes until a skewer comes out clean

Leave in the tin for 15 minutes before turning out to cool
