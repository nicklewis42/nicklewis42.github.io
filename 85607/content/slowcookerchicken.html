Slow cooker Chicken

Introduction

This is a way to cook a (small) whole chicken in the slow cooker, you can use the result just like a roast chicken but it will be more moist and tender. Bear in mind if you are one who likes to eat the skin it'll be very pale and bland.

DescriptionQuantityUOMComment
Chicken1eaA whole chicken of a size to fit comfortably in your slow cooker
Onion1A medium sized onion, you don't need a big one.

Method

Slice the onion into rings and jumble them into the slow cooker.

Put the chicken on top and add 100ml or so of water

Cook on low until done, experience says 4 hours but the interior should reach 70C juices run clear and so on.

Discard the onions and use the stock to make gravy if you wish.

Final Comments

The onions are as much to make a space for the water under the bird as anything but they do seem to add a little flavour.
