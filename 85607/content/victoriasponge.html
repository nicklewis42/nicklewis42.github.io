Victoria Sponge

Introduction

You can go by the weights below but you will get more consistent results by weighing the eggs in their shells and use the same weight of caster sugar, margarine and flour.

DescriptionQuantityUOMComment
Caster sugar8oz
Margarine8oz
SR flour8oz
Eggs4each
Milk a little
Butter or margarine100g
Icing sugar200g

Method

Beat Marg and caster sugar together.

Mix in a little of the flour and then add the eggs.

Mix in the remaining flour.

Add enough milk to give a soft dropping consistency.

Divide between two lined sandwich tins.

Bake at gas 3.5 for 38 minutes.

Fill with butter cream made by blending the icing sugar into the margarine.

Final Comments

The key to getting a light well risen sponge is the beating of the fat and sugar together at the start. After that everything should be done gently to avoid driving out the air.

If you prefer to use a mechanical mixer you can put all the ingredients in and mix in one step, the power of the mixer will ensure the mix is aerated.
