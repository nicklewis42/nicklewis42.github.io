Air Fried Jacket Potatoes

Introduction

Potatoes - You want a size so two or three will fit the Fryer.

Oil and salt for rubbing.

Method

Set the fryer to 200 C.

Thoroughly clean the potatoes.

Rub the skins in the salt and oil.

Put the potatoes in the fryer for 30 minutes.

Turn the spuds and cook for a further 20 minutes.
