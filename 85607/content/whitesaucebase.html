White sauce base

Introduction

This is a basic white sauce, add parsley, cheese or whatever to customise it.

DescriptionQuantityUOMComment
Butter20g
Flour (plain)20g
Milk320ml

Method

Gently melt the butter in a pan and stir in the flour to make a smooth rue.

Carefully whisk in the milk, watch you don't add the milk too slowly and the heat to fast, you don't want it to cook too fast.

Gently heat without boiling until it thickens.

If it's not just a plain white sauce finally add the other ingredients.
