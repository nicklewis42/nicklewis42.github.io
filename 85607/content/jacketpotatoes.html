Jacket Potatoes

Introduction

Potatoes - For best results big but not too big.

Oil and salt for rubbing.

Method

Preheat the oven to gas 7.

Thoroughly clean the potatoes.

Rub the skins in the salt and oil.

Put the potatoes in the oven either directly on the shelf or on a perforated sheet.

After twenty minutes turn down to mark 5.

After reducing the heat expect them to take around an hour but test with a fork or skewer.

You can turn them once or twice dering cooking but it's not essential.
