Rice (Absorption method)

Introduction

This is how you cook white rice by the absorption method.

DescriptionQuantityUOMComment
Dried rice75gper person
Water Twice the rice by volume

Method

Wash the rice thoroughy in a sieve under the cold tap.

Put the rice and water in a saucepan and bring to a good strong boil.

As soon as the rice is boiling well give it one quick stir and turn the gas down low, put on a tight fitting lid and leave well alone for 10 minutes exactly.

Once the ten minutes has elapsed turn the heat off, leave well alone for a further five minutes.

Serve!
