Pancakes

Introduction

Makes about a portion or five small pancakes, scale as required.

DescriptionQuantityUOMComment
Plain Flour50g
Eggs1ea
Milk100ml

Method

Beat the ingredients together until you have a smooth thin batter.

Heat your pan to a high heat with just a trace of oil.

Add just enough batter to spread over the bottom of the pan by rocking the pan around.

Fry for a short time turning once, the pancake should be lightly golden on both sides in about a minute.

Final Comments

Serve however you want; rolled, stacked stuffed; with lemon juice and sugar, marmalade, syrup etc...
