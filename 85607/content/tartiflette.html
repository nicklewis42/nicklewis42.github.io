Tartiflette

Introduction

You can substitute with any kind of cheese and still get a stunning result. Don't skip the wine its an integral part of the flavour.

DescriptionQuantityUOMComment
Potatos1kg
Bacon lardons8oz
Shallots2
Garlick1Clove
White wine100ml
Double cream200ml
Reblechon cheese1lb

Method

boil potatoes until almost cooked but stll firm enough to slice. Alternatively slice before cooking and steam until tender.

Fry the shallottes, garlick and bacon.

Slice the potatos and layer the solid ingredients alternately into a gratin dish.

Deglaze the pan frying pan with the wine and pour over the other ingedients.

Puor the cream over.

Spread the cheese on top.

10-15 minutes at gas 7 will melt and brown the cheese.
