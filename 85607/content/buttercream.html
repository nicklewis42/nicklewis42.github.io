Butter cream

Introduction

Enough to fill on large sponge cake, scale as needed.

DescriptionQuantityUOMComment
Butter100gSoften the butter first
icing sugar200gSifted
Milk As required

Method

Beat the softened butter until smooth

Beat in the icing sugar a little at a time.

At this point it will probably be a little too stiff, this is where you add any liquids, vanilla essence, colouring, and then enough milk to make it smooth and the texture you need
