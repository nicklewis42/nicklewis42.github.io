Egg ea

Method

Grease a ramikin with butter

Crack an egg into the ramikin

Add a little cream and grated cheese or other topping

Air Fry at 150C for 10 minutes

Final Comments

Tweak the cooking time to taste it'll be a little less if you don't use any topping. You can also do two eggs in a bigger ramikin which might need a little longer cooking time.
