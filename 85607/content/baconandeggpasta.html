Bacon and Egg Pasta

DescriptionQuantityUOMComment
Bacon4rashers
spaghetti250g
Grated parmesan50g
Cream150ml
Egg2each
Smoked Paprika teaspoonoptional, makes a smokier flavour.

Method

Cut the bacon into thin strips, fry until crisp.

Boil the spaghetti until tender drain and return to pan

Beat the eggs, cheese cream and paprika in a bowl then add the bacon.

Pour over the hot pasta, toss well to mix, and return to the heat for a minute or two until thickened.
