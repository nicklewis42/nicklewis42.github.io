Method

Chop the veg to a size that works for you but that should be pretty fine. Especially for the onion.

fry the veg until it softens somewhat in a little oil with the Garlick.

Add the pasta and veg to the boiling stock and simmer until everything including the pasta is tender.

Final Comments

There are no quantities in this recipe yet but a little of everything is what works with enough stock to make it into quite a thick soup.
