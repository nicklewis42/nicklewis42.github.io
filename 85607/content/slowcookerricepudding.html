Slow Cooker Rice Pudding

Introduction

Replace 150g of the milk with cream if you want a richer pudding.

DescriptionQuantityUOMComment
Arborio rice100g
Milk650g
Sugar40gCaster sugar or brown sugar
Vanilla flavouring1tsp

Method

Butter the slow cooker

Mix all the liquids and the sugar together and stir until the sugar is dissolved

Add all the ingredients and give a quick stir

Cook on low for 3.5 hours, stir once during cooking
