Method

Process oats to break them down a little, don

Put the corflakes in a bowl and break them up a little to make small cornflakes.

Thoroughly mix the oats, cornflakes and sugar in a bowl.

Melt the butter and syrup together in a pan, not too hot, just enough to melt the butter.

Add the melted butter and syrup to the bowl and mix well.

Press really firmly into a baking tray finishing with the back of a spoon.

Bake for about 15 minutes at gas 5.
