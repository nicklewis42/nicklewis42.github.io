Rock Cakes

Introduction

Simplest possible cakes!

DescriptionQuantityUOMComment
Self raising flour225g
Caster sugar75g
Baking powder1tsp
Butter125g
Dried fruit150g
Egg1
Milk1tbsp
Vanilla extract2tsp

Method

Mix flour, sugar and baking powder in a bowl.

Cut butter into pieces and rub into mix to a breadcrumb consistency.

Mix in the dried fruit.

In a new bowl beat together the egg, milk and vanilla.

Add the egg mixture to the main bowl and mix with a spoon. It should just come together but still be lumpy.

Put dollops onto a parchment lined baking sheet, this quantity makes 6 to 9.

Bake in preheated oven gas 4 for 20-30 minutes.
