Air Fried Chips

Introduction

The Air Fryer makes great chips although it does take a bit more time and effort.

Don't skimp on the cooking time, they should be well browned and crispy.

Method

Set the fryer to 180 C.

Peel the potatoes and cut roughly into chips, aim for lots of angles rather than neatness.

Soak the chips for ten minutes in colde water.

Drain and remove excess water with Kitchen roll.

Toss the chips in a little oil, they should be coated in oil.

Air fry the chips turning them every five minutes, they should be done in 20 to 30 minutes.
