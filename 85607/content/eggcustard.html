Egg Custard

Introduction

This recipe makes a nice sweet egg custard for filling tarts or as a desert in itself.

DescriptionQuantityUOMComment
Milk300mlFull fat will be creamier
Egg2Probably large, if tiny use three
Sugar25gAny kind of sugar as it will completely dissolve, brown is interesting
Vanilla extract1tsp
Ground Nutmeg1tsp

Method

Separate one of the eggs and discard the white.

Beat all the cold ingredients together.

Split between two ramikins if this is a desert on it's own.

Microwave on 30% for 14 minutes until set.

Allow to cool and then refrigerate for at least two hours.
