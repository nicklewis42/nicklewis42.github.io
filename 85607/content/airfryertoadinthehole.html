Air Fryer Toad in the Hole

DescriptionQuantityUOMComment
Milk100ml
Plain flour70g
Egg1
Sausages4(or maybe six?)

Method

Put the sausages in a large ramikin with enough oil to cover the bottom and cook at 200C for three minutes

Beat flour, egg and milk to make a smooth batter.

Add batter and return to the air fryer until cooked, probably about 10 mins.
