Blondies

Introduction

Like brownies but with no chocolate

DescriptionQuantityUOMComment
butter50g
peanut butter soft or crunchy200g
vanilla essence1tsp
light brown golden sugar175g
egg1
white cooking chocolate (optional)75g
sr flour125g

Method

Beat butter and peanut butter till soft and smooth.

Add vanilla essence.

Add sugar and egg and mix well.

Mix in flour

Add milk to a soft consistency

Chop up chocolate into small pieces, add to mix and stir well.

Butter tin and line.

Spread mix evenly in tin.

Put in oven for about 25 - 30 minutes at gas mark 4.

Cut in the tin into squares and leave to cool, decorate as required and lift out of the tin
