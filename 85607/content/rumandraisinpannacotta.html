Rum and Raisin Pannacotta

Introduction

Panacotta base here with christmas spices, rum and raisins. Really tastes of christmas pud.

DescriptionQuantityUOMComment
Double cream300g
Full fat milk200g
Raisins 
Mixed spice tsp
Cinammon tsp
Dark Rum 
Gelatin Enough to set a pint or so

Method

Pour enough rum to cover over the fruit, leave to soak up the liquid for a couple of hours or overnight.

Dissolve the gelatin in 100ml of water in accordance with the instructions.

Put the milk and cream on the heat and bring to boiling point.

Add the spices and stir well, take off the heat.

Add the gelatin and fruit; stir well.

Pour into ramikins, the fruit will all be at the bottom, just share it out at the end.

Leave to set overnight in the fridge.
