Quiche

Introduction

The recipe includes suggestions for the filling but anything which will not mix with the eggs can be used.

Method

Roll out pastry and shape into lightly greased flan tin, prick the bottom of the case with a fork.

Leave case in Fridge for at least 1 hour.

Blind the case with beans and bake for 10 to 20 minutes Gas 6.

Remove the blinding and return to oven for five minutes.

Beat the eggs and brush the inside of the case.

Back in the oven for another five minutes.

Chop and cook some combination of bacon sausages and mushrooms to taste.

Make the custard by adding an equal volume of milk to the eggs and beating together, replace some of the milk with the cream for a richer custard.

Distribute the cooked meats and some chopped or grated cheese in the case.

Add the cream and then full fat milk to the same weight as the eggs.

Beat the custard and then pour over the ingredients in the case.

Back into the oven at Gas 3 until set and slightly browned on top, about 40 minutes.
